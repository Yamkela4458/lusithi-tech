About Me

Yamkela Lusithi is an English Language Educator and Digital Learning Enthusiast currently pursuing a B.Ed Honours in Language, Literacies and Literature at the University of Johannesburg. His work focuses on the intersection of education, artificial intelligence, and digital literacy, designing technology-enhanced learning systems that improve access to literacy and Education. With a background in technology and pedagogy, he develops AI-supported learning interfaces, blended learning environments, and research-informed digital education tools. His goal is to ensure that educational technology strengthens teaching practice while improving learner engagement, digital competence, and academic achievement.
