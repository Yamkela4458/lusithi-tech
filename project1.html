Project Title: Workplace Communication Module | Articulate Rise 360 Case Study

The Challenge

In modern workplace environments, ineffective communication remains a leading cause of conflict, reduced productivity, and misunderstandings. Adult learners often struggle to transfer theoretical communication knowledge into real-world professional contexts, particularly when training lacks relevance, interactivity, and practical application.

The Solution

I designed a high-fidelity, scenario-based eLearning module using Articulate Rise 360. The course focuses on authentic workplace situations, allowing learners to engage in decision-making tasks that mirror real communication challenges. The module supports learners in developing practical communication strategies through guided interaction and immediate feedback.

Instructional Design Strategy

  • Adult Learning Principles (Andragogy): The module is grounded in relevance and real-world application, ensuring learners can immediately connect content to workplace experiences.
  • Scenario-Based Learning: Learners engage with realistic communication dilemmas, promoting active problem-solving and deeper cognitive processing.
  • Bloom's Taxonomy Alignment: The course progresses from Understanding (communication concepts) to Applying (selecting appropriate responses) and Evaluating (reflecting on outcomes and effectiveness).
  • Cognitive Load Management: Content is segmented into short, focused sections with interactive checkpoints to maintain engagement and prevent overload.
  • Feedback-Driven Learning: Immediate, contextual feedback is provided to reinforce correct decisions and guide learners toward improved communication strategies.

Tools Used

  • Articulate Rise 360: For responsive course design and interactive content development.
  • Scenario-Based Instructional Design: For creating realistic, decision-driven learning experiences.
  • Adult Learning Theory: To ensure relevance, autonomy, and practical application.
